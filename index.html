See what we’re about



“HOT-DOGS” It’s an educational website that provides plenty of educational resources, summary notes and well explained previous exams keys, an organized and easy-to-access content. The website is made for ENSEM’s students, but other college students may find it useful as well, it’s free to use. Welcome and we are so glad you’re here!.
This website is a school project, we made it as a solution for new students to get access to these resources easily because is difficult to search around and ask many students if they have the information or not and collect it.
This website will contain resources for computer science, electrical engineering, mechanical Engineering and Industrial Engineering.
